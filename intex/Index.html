Geography

Salem districts covers an area of 5245 sq. km.
It is bounded by the districts of Namakkal, Dharmapuri, Erode, and Kallakurichi.
The name Salem is thought to be derived from Sailam (hills) on account of being surrounded by hills

Law enforcement

Law and order is maintained by the Salem district Tamil Nadu Police, headed by a Superintendent of Police
Special units include prohibition enforcement, district crime, social justice and human rights, district crime records and a district-level special branch
Salem district is in charge of the central prison at Hastampatti. It's one of the oldest jails and is able to host upto 1431 house prisoners
